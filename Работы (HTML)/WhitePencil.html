White Pencil

White pencil is the most mysterious of all the other colored pencils, few people think about its meaning...

Ingredients

  • Titanium dioxide or zinc whitewash
  • Wax or oil
  • Kaolin (white clay)
  • Wood for the body (cedar, linden)
  • Eraser on the other end of the pencil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Buy a white pencil
  2. Pull out from box
  3. Take it in your leading hand
  4. Start creating
